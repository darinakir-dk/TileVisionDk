# 1. Business Requirements

## 1.1 Background

When choosing ceramic tiles or flooring materials, customers often struggle to imagine how the selected materials will look in their own rooms. This uncertainty leads to:

- Longer decision-making processes
- Increased dependence on store consultants and designers
- Higher abandonment rates
- Lower purchase confidence

Most existing visualization services require a designer to manually create a 3D model of the room, making the process expensive and time-consuming.

**TileVision** aims to simplify and automate this experience through AI-powered visualization.

---

## 1.2 Business Opportunity

Advances in AI image recognition enable retailers to automate interior material visualization.

TileVision helps tile and flooring retailers:

- Increase sales conversion
- Increase average order value
- Reduce consultants' workload
- Improve customer experience
- Differentiate from competitors

---

## 1.3 Business Goals & Success Criteria

| Business Goal | Success Criteria |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Reduce customer uncertainty | 80% of users successfully create a visualization |
| Increase sales conversion | +15% conversion rate |
| Increase average order value | +10% average order value |
| Reduce decision-making time | Most users create their first visualization in under 3 minutes |

---

## 1.4 Customer & Market Needs

Customers want to:

- Visualize materials in their own room
- Compare multiple design options
- Receive material combination recommendations
- View only products currently in stock
- Make confident decisions without purchasing an expensive design project

---

## 1.5 Business Risks

- AI surface recognition accuracy
- Customer trust in generated visualizations
- Integration complexity with retailer catalogs
- Emerging competitors
- Poor-quality uploaded photos

# 2. Vision of the Solution

## 2.1 Vision Statement

TileVision is an AI-powered web application for tile and flooring retailers.

Customers can:

- Upload a room photo
- Automatically detect walls and floors using AI
- Apply materials from the retailer's catalog
- Compare multiple design options
- Receive AI-powered recommendations
- Share the selected design with a store consultant

---

## 2.2 Key Features

- Room photo upload
- AI surface detection
- Manual surface correction
- Tile & flooring catalog
- Realistic material visualization
- Design comparison
- AI material recommendations
- Design sharing with consultants

---

## 2.3 Assumptions & Dependencies

### Assumptions

- Customers have a room photo
- Retail catalog is up to date
- AI accurately detects most surfaces

### Dependencies

- AI image recognition service
- Product catalog database
- Cloud image storage

# 3. Scope & Limitations

## 3.1 MVP Scope

The MVP includes:

- Room photo upload
- AI surface detection
- Manual boundary editing
- Product catalog browsing
- Tile & flooring visualization
- Design comparison
- Compatible material recommendations
- Sharing selected design with a consultant

---

## 3.2 Future Releases

Planned future functionality:

- Design inspiration library
- Budget-based recommendations
- Lighting simulation
- Grout color selection
- Automatic tile orientation optimization
- Material waste optimization
- AR visualization
- Automatic material quantity estimation
- Mobile application (iOS & Android)

---

## 3.3 Out of Scope

TileVision does **not** include:

- Professional interior design services
- Furniture or plumbing planning
- Room remodeling
- BIM/CAD model creation
- Construction calculations
- Online ordering & payments
- Delivery or installation management
- Inventory or ERP management
- Products unavailable in the retailer catalog
